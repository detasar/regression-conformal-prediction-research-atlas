Evidence Scope

  • The study reports empirical observations from this audited experiment surface.
  • It does not establish production advice, a universal winner, population-level group inference, or bounded-support validity.
  • The Venn-Abers result is about the evaluated regression bridge, not the broader Venn-Abers literature.
